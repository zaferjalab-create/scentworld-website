About Us

Crafting Atmosphere Across Canada

Scent World Canada is Atlantic Canada's premier scent marketing company — bringing international fragrance expertise and localized service to luxury hotels, spas, restaurants, retail spaces, and discerning homes across the country.

Our Story

Built on a Belief in The Power of Atmosphere

Scent World Canada was founded on a simple observation: the world's greatest hospitality brands had figured out something that most businesses overlook entirely. Scent is the most powerful and overlooked element of brand experience.

From the Ritz-Carlton's signature lobby aroma to Singapore Airlines' cabin fragrance to Apple's perfectly controlled retail atmosphere — the most memorable brands invest in scent because they understand memory is built through emotion, and emotion travels through aroma.

Yet across Canada — particularly in the Atlantic provinces — this discipline remained almost entirely untapped. We founded Scent World Canada to change that. Our mission is to bring world-class scent marketing technology, premium fragrance development, and strategic consulting to every Canadian business that values its guest experience.

Our Philosophy

Atmosphere is the Brand Made Felt

Every space tells a story. Architecture sets the scene, lighting paints the mood, music sets the tempo — but scent is what makes the story live in memory. We believe atmosphere is the most under-leveraged competitive advantage in modern business, and we exist to help our clients harness it.
